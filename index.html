Services

MADE-TO-ORDER SERVICE

Our made-to-order jewelry service allows customers to design their own unique pieces using a range of materials and working with skilled craftsmen. Whether it's a personalized engagement ring, a customized pendant, or custom earrings, our service allows customers to create a special and meaningful piece of jewelry. Our team provides excellent customer service and attention to detail to ensure high quality.

REDESIGN services

Our jewelry redesign service allows customers to update or transform old or inherited pieces. Our skilled craftsmen based in Legapzi can help customers create a modern, stylish design using high quality materials. Customers can keep sentimental value or create something new and unique. Our service allows customers to create special, meaningful jewelry.

GOLD AND SILVER PLATING

Our gold plating service allows customers to give their jewelry a luxurious, refined look by adding a thin layer of gold to its surface. Our skilled craftsmen use only the highest quality materials and techniques to ensure that the gold plating is durable and long-lasting.
